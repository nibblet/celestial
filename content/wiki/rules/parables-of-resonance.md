## Canon Dossier

**Aliases:** vault-echo symbolism

Parables of Resonance are rare, symbolic vision-events experienced by crew members during moments of high emotional or harmonic alignment. They are not hallucinations, AI simulations, or dreams. Rather, they are encoded moral transmissions—deliberately embedded by the Ancients as part of their philosophical legacy. Unlike data, parables are interpretive experiences—each shaped by the consciousness and emotional composition of the receiver. They often echo mythic or spiritual forms, and some subtly mirror ancient human parables (e.g., those attributed to Jesus in early Christian texts), suggesting a long arc of symbolic recurrence seeded through time. They function as a bridge between Ancient truth and modern comprehension; mirror the emotional or moral conflict of the recipient; convey foundational ethics without exposition or doctrine; reinforce core themes: alignment over authority, invitation over inheritance, truth as living resonance; and symbolically link Ancient resonance teachings to Earth's religious and philosophical traditions. They are written in mythic, poetic tone, appear interstitially within scenes—italicized and unframed by character awareness, typically unattributed and untimed—no log ID or mission record. Not recalled or discussed directly at first; their meaning unfolds through character behavior, ALARA's shifts, or vault reactions. Rare: ~3–5 per book to preserve mystique and potency.
