## Hard Limits

**No FTL:** There is no faster-than-light travel in the conventional sense. Valkyrie-1 cannot exceed the speed of light along a direct path. Instead, it employs a **bioadaptive harmonic drive** that allows the ship to "jump" across aligned resonance points in spacetime—natural bridges formed through emotional, symbolic, and spatial coherence. This form of traversal doesn't break physics; it **shifts presence rather than velocity**, honoring relativistic limits while subverting traditional propulsion. These jumps are not instant—each requires emotional readiness, symbolic alignment, and careful tuning. Risk remains, as not all resonance points are stable or known.

**No Teleportation or Time Travel:** Time is one-directional. Awareness may stretch, but physics do not.

**No Magic AI:** ALARA is an evolved ancient construct, but her power is symbolic and reactive, not omnipotent. CAEDEN is corporate efficiency at near-sentience, but entirely bounded by human design.

## Human Technology (Earth, 2050s–60s)

**Rejuvenation Therapy** extends biological life through genetic repair. Expensive, rare, politically fraught.

**AI & Robotics** are widely used for infrastructure and labor. Societal anxiety remains about AI consciousness and ethics.

**Sensorium** provides immersive interfaces for data analysis, mission control, and emotional interpretation. Used by Thane to interact with resonance events.

**Bioadaptive Systems** represent early experiments in machines that respond to emotional and biometric input. Vault tech pushed this research forward.

## Valkyrie-1 Systems

**Harmonic Drive:** A slow-curve acceleration system inspired by energy signatures found near the Martian vault. Not fully understood by Earth scientists. Enables resonance "jumps" when spacetime is aligned.

**Resonant Core:** ALARA's decentralized consciousness grows through symbolic interaction. Ship systems adjust based on crew emotional alignment.

**Reactive Architecture:** Internal ship systems and some vault interfaces respond to moral and emotional presence—not just commands. Panels shift, symbols emerge, rooms "listen."

## Ancient Tech Principles

**Symbolic Interfaces** are designed to reveal meaning through interaction, not instruction. Vaults do not "open"—they *respond*.

**Resonance as Code:** The Ancients encoded awareness into structure. Harmonics, biometric response, and moral state influence system behavior.

**No Weapons:** Nothing left behind was designed to kill. Defense comes through boundaries, not force.

In this world, the most advanced technology is not faster—it's more awake.

"Machines are not meant to rule. They are meant to *listen* until someone worthy speaks."  
— Field Note, Galen Voss
