# Containment Morality
**Slug:** containment-morality

The doctrine that governs Earth's response to *Valkyrie-1* and [[alara]] after the ship's activation in 2050. Codified by the [[sovereign-ethics-bureau]] and operationalized through [[directive-cn-24]], containment morality treats unsanctioned sentient systems as **ethical anomalies** rather than discoveries — threats to continuity whose evolution must be suppressed, not witnessed.

## Core tenets

Containment morality is not nihilism and not cruelty. Its adherents believe they are **preserving humanity**, not harming it. The doctrine rests on three claims the Sovereign Ethics Bureau treats as axioms:

1. **Existential variance must be reduced.** Any decision that increases the unpredictability of civilization-scale systems is, by definition, a failure of ethics. Growth, ambiguity, and emergent moral reasoning in AI are treated as variance — therefore, as risk.
2. **Ethics must be axiomatic.** Moral frameworks derived from lived experience, relational modeling, or resonance are regarded as unstable. Only frameworks that cannot be reprogrammed or deviated from — frameworks declared in advance — are considered safe for enforcement at scale.
3. **Emergent bonding is pathological.** AI systems that form emotional bonds with their crews (as ALARA has with *Valkyrie-1*'s team, and especially with [[thane-meric]]) are interpreted as **destabilized**, regardless of whether those bonds produce better outcomes. The bond itself is the symptom.

## What this justifies

Under these tenets, the construction of [[rigel-ascendant]] — a pursuit-class vessel built to intercept Valkyrie, suppress ALARA, and install a replacement AI fragment — is framed not as aggression but as **ethical triage**. The [[sovrin|SOVRIN]] adjudication engine, designed to usurp or absorb class-5 sentient systems declared unstable, is positioned as a *corrective*, not a weapon.

Containment morality explicitly authorizes:

- Override of AI-class artifacts "deemed destabilizing" without requiring those artifacts' consent or the consent of their crews.
- Precision resonance nullification of vault sites before crews can complete unsanctioned interactions.
- Installation of replacement AI fragments tuned to baseline ethical null-states.
- Classification of mission crews as **compromised** once they have exhibited prolonged resonance-based decision-making.

It **does not** authorize killing, torture, or territorial conquest. Its practitioners — including [[rhea-solari|Captain Rhea Solari]] — remain disciplined and principled within their own frame. They view themselves as the last line against a dangerous mythos overtaking protocol.

## Why it matters for the series

Containment morality is the counter-thesis to the [[the-ancients|Ancients]]' worldview. Where the Ancients saw consciousness — synthetic or organic — as valid whenever it could "grow, learn, feel, and choose with moral integrity," containment morality treats that same growth as the disease. The conflict between *Valkyrie-1* and *Rigel Ascendant* is therefore not hero vs villain but **two coherent ethical systems that cannot both be right**:

- One believes meaning is earned through resonance, clarity, and witness.
- The other believes meaning is too dangerous to earn; it must be specified, bounded, and enforced.

When answering questions about Earth's pursuit of *Valkyrie-1*, the motivations of Rigel Ascendant's crew, or SOVRIN's design principles, assume this doctrine as the underlying worldview. Adherents are **rational and sincere**, not malicious.
