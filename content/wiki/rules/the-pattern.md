**Aliases:** Pattern, universal consciousness

The Ancients inferred the presence of a universal consciousness—not a deity, but a distributed awareness throughout the cosmos. Some called it the Pattern. Others, in later human history, would call it God. The Ancients believed the difference was semantic.
