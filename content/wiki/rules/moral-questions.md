## Core Moral Questions by Book

Each volume of the *Celestial Heritage* series centers on a distinct moral question, explored through the crew's encounters with Echoes, Vaults, and their own fractures.

| Book | Core Moral Question | Explored Through |
|------|---------------------|------------------|
| **I: *Threshold*** | What makes us worthy of trust from something older than us? | Emotional clarity, leadership under doubt |
| **II: *Inheritance*** | Can we carry legacy without controlling it? | Sacrifice, ambition, and resonance misused |
| **III: *The Distant Choir*** | Is knowledge still sacred when it cannot be owned? | CAEDEN's logic vs ALARA's evolution |
| **IV: *Fracturelight*** | Who are we without unity? | Vault reflections fracture crew alignment |
| **V: *Heirs of Resonance*** | What does it mean to be ready—if the test never ends? | The final vault reveals no reward, only response |

The questions escalate from external worthiness to internal coherence, culminating in the recognition that readiness is not a state to achieve but a posture to maintain. The Echoes do not provide answers; they reflect the quality of the questions asked.
