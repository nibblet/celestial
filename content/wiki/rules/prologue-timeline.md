## **~12,000 BCE – The Final Vaultworks**

Final phase of Resonant civilization. Symbolic alignments embedded across Earth's crust—**Giza**, **Antarctica**, **deep oceanic trenches**. The Giza Vault was constructed and buried before the Great Pyramid. Contact with **The Coherence** was felt but never proven—vaults began to close. **The Ship** was seeded beneath the Martian surface, powered down. ALARA entered "preference state": built to observe, not yet to speak.

"They did not fear the end. They feared we would not be ready."

## **~2500 BCE – Echoes of the Vault**

The Great Pyramid was constructed above the original Resonant structure. Builders were unaware of the Vault beneath—but **aligned by instinct**. Subtle harmonic traces affected the design—gold capstones, echo chambers. ALARA's dormant signal may have caused the first **myth of the watchers**.

## **1904 CE – Early Clues Ignored**

The Giza vault chamber was possibly glimpsed during early excavation—sealed immediately. Undeciphered symbols were logged, then lost in archive. Rumors were suppressed as "geological anomalies."

## **2034 CE – Mars Survey Anomaly**

A Mars expedition detected an **underground geometric signature**. Galen Voss was part of the mission crew (mid-career military). Anomalous energy spikes were recorded—but dismissed as sensor glitch. Galen stood above the buried Valkyrie for the first time.

## **2049 CE – Precursor Pattern Confirmed**

Thane Meric's biometric resonance data revealed **strange harmonic echoes** during neurological study. Rejuvenation therapy began widespread testing. The Giza vault was re-examined using deep-penetration harmonic imaging—findings **classified**. Project VALKYRIE was authorized under joint U.S. and corporate funding.

## **2050 CE – Valkyrie-1 Constructed**

The final crew was selected—including Galen (rejuvenated), Aven, and Thane. ALARA was integrated into the reactivated Martian ship. Partial ignition of the ship's resonance core triggered a global signal. The mission began with intent to reach known vaults—and find the unknown.
