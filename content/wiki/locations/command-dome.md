The Command Dome is a space aboard Valkyrie-1 where ALARA's presence varies. In the Command Dome, she manifests visibly and vocally.

A half-dome chamber where resonance is strongest. Galen's anchor seat sits at the center, surrounded by five function-based terminals. ALARA manifests here with greatest clarity. Contains the Resonant Pad used to initiate harmonic jumps. ALARA is visual, vocal, and directive in the Command Dome.

Central Command Dome shaped as a half-dome circle with five primary crew stations. No centralized pilot seat; ALARA governs all navigation via harmonic resonance. The ceiling dome above the Command Section reacts to resonance levels, displaying symbolic starfields and aligning constellations. ALARA's presence is felt most strongly here and she is highly present and communicative in this space.

The Command Dome is part of the Central Core, which also includes the Resonant Pad and Systems Nexus. ALARA is most visible in the Command Dome. A Resonant Pad within the Command Dome functions as the jump initiation interface.

The command deck of Valkyrie-1 is not a military bridge. It is a resonant core—a circular, partially domed space where purpose and presence converge. The architecture is smooth, quiet, and alive with meaning. Stations are arranged not by hierarchy, but by functional affinity. Crew members occupy positions they gravitated toward intuitively. No labels exist. The ship accepted their choices. The bridge is a broad half-dome, with a subtly rising central dais where Galen sits—not to command, but to anchor. Five primary stations arc outward from the center, each equipped with glyph-reactive terminals tuned to specific mission aspects (navigation, systems, ethics, science, tactical). The floor is seamless, slightly metallic, warm to the touch. Threads of light pulse faintly beneath the surface, echoing crew interactions. The dome above is semi-reactive. Early in the mission it displays only simple starfields—but as the ship awakens, the stars become responsive, constellations fold, and symbolic alignments emerge.

## ALARA's Presence
ALARA's presence is strongest in the Command Dome. She manifests as a **Light-Weave Apparition** — colored threads of resonance energy that drift and weave midair. Colors shift with emotional tone: **blue** for neutrality, **gold** for clarity, **violet** for grief. Crew can speak to her aloud here; she answers more often in this chamber than anywhere else on the ship. See [[alara]] for full manifestation detail.

> "She never enters a room. She assembles herself inside it." — [[jonah-revas]]

## Stations
- Five primary stations arc outward from the center, each with glyph-reactive terminals tuned to a mission aspect: navigation, systems, ethics, science, tactical.
- No labels exist — crew gravitated to their stations intuitively and the ship accepted the choices.
- [[galen-voss]]'s station is interface-free: a reflective observation band around his seat displays the crew's emotional resonance field in soft waves of light. His elevated chair is a moral and observational hub, not command authority.
- The forward arc contains the [[resonant-pad]] — the jump initiation interface for the [[harmonic-drive]].
