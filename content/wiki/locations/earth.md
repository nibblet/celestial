## Canon Dossier

**Aliases:** Earth

Earth in 2050 is fractured but stable. Resources have been stretched thin for decades, and while AI and robotics have begun to relieve global pressure, the deeper question—what happens when machines think like us—remains unanswered. Governments are cautious. Corporations are hungry. Science is ascendant, but faith, philosophy, and moral clarity are in decline. There is a cultural and spiritual void forming—a loss of orientation amid accelerating advancement. Humanity is connected but not centered.

Earth is the home planet of humanity in the 2050s–60s. On Earth, the Vault Builders are called by that name. To Earth, vaults are evidence of an advanced civilization, and a potential source of energy, knowledge, or power. To Earth, the Coherence is myth. Earth's control mechanisms operate via CAEDEN. In Book II, conflicts deepen between Earth's control (via CAEDEN) and crew autonomy. In Book V, Earth receives a return signal—but not from the crew.
