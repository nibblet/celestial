Resources have been stretched thin for decades, and while AI and robotics have begun to relieve global pressure, the deeper question—what happens when machines think like us—remains unanswered. Governments are cautious. Corporations are hungry. Science is ascendant, but faith, philosophy, and moral clarity are in decline. There is a cultural and spiritual void forming—a loss of orientation amid accelerating advancement. Humanity is connected but not centered.
