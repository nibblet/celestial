## Description
The Resonant Pad is the jump initiation interface for Valkyrie-1's [[harmonic-drive]], mounted near the forward arc of the [[command-dome]]. It is a handprint station that activates only through emotional coherence — when [[thane-meric]] or another resonance-aligned crew member places a hand on it, the pad glows and pulses, a rare event always observed in silence.

The ship has no pilot. [[alara]] governs navigation, responding to crew directives and vault resonance patterns; the Resonant Pad is the symbolic surface through which a jump is offered — not commanded. Jumps can only occur when alignment conditions are met (emotional, moral, and crew-focused), and not all resonance points are stable or known.

This non-traditional navigation structure — relying on emotional resonance and symbolic alignment rather than manual control — is central to Valkyrie-1's guidance logic and should never be described as a pilot console.

### Notable uses
- **CH10 Convergence Window** — Thane experiences emotional convergence at the pad during ALARA's silence, triggering delivery of "[[the-vessel-and-the-thread]]" parable from [[vault-002]].
