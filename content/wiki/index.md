# Celestial Wiki Index

Canonical index for the Celestial companion wiki.

## Story Spine
- Chapters are tracked as `CH01`, `CH02`, ... and unlocked by reader progress.
- Mission logs are indexed under `content/raw/mission_logs_inventory.json` and browsable in `/mission-logs`.

## Entity Collections
- Characters: `content/wiki/characters/`
- Character arcs: `content/wiki/arcs/characters/`
- Artifacts: `content/wiki/artifacts/`
- Locations: `content/wiki/locations/`
- Factions: `content/wiki/factions/`
- Rules: `content/wiki/rules/`
- Themes: `content/wiki/themes/`

## Reading Experience Rules
- Locked chapter/entity content is hidden until the reader reaches the chapter.
- Re-reader mode can reveal all content from profile settings.
