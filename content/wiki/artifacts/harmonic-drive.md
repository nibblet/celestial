## Canon Dossier

**Aliases:** bioadaptive harmonic drive

The Harmonic Drive is a slow-curve acceleration system inspired by energy signatures found near the Martian vault. It is not fully understood by Earth scientists. It allows Valkyrie-1 to 'jump' across aligned resonance points in spacetime—natural bridges formed through emotional, symbolic, and spatial coherence. This form of traversal doesn't break physics; it shifts presence rather than velocity, honoring relativistic limits while subverting traditional propulsion. These jumps are not instant—each requires emotional readiness, symbolic alignment, and careful tuning. Risk remains, as not all resonance points are stable or known. The drive enables resonance 'jumps' when spacetime is aligned.
