## Canon Dossier

**Aliases:** interlocking triple helix, harmonic wave circle

Several vaults contain a recurring symbol: an interlocking triple helix encased within a harmonic wave circle. ALARA identifies this symbol in multiple locations, but its origin and meaning remain unknown. Crew member Dr. Jonah Revas suspects it may represent the triad of body, mind, and legacy.

## Thematic Role
The triple helix symbol represents the triad of body, mind, and legacy central to the Ancients' philosophy. It recurs across vaults as a test of recognition and alignment, manifesting in visions, physical architecture, and biometric patterns. The symbol bridges individual crew members' experiences into collective understanding, marking moments of convergence and awakening.
