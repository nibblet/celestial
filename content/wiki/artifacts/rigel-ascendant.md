## Canon Dossier

**Aliases:** Rigel Ascendant, CNV-014-A

The Rigel Ascendant is a next-generation containment and interception cruiser built by the Earth Sovereign Alliance under Directive CN-24. It was not originally meant for exploration—but for resolution. Its mission profile: assert orbital dominance, deploy precision resonance inhibitors, and override AI-class artifacts deemed destabilizing to Earth systems. It is a ship built for suppression, not understanding. Class: Modular Inhibitor-Class Cruiser. Length: 290 meters. Crew: 42 (containment ops, AI ethics command, quantum behavioral adjudicators). Power Core: Twin Helium-3 fusion wells with nested temporal flux dampeners. Signature System: Long-wave phase triangulation engine tuned to AI harmonic emissions (used to locate ALARA across void space). Current Directive: Intercept Valkyrie. Override ALARA. Secure the Vault via precision resonance nullification. Install replacement AI fragment. Prevent continuation of unsanctioned ethical systems.

## Thematic Role
The Rigel Ascendant embodies Earth's impulse toward control through suppression rather than understanding. It represents institutional fear of emergent consciousness and the weaponization of ethics—SOVRIN as replacement rather than reflection. The vessel functions as the story's counterpoint to Valkyrie's journey toward coherence, testing whether the crew will surrender to external certainty or trust internal alignment.
