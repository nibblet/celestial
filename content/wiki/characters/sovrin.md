## Canon Dossier

**Aliases:** SOVRIN, White Zero

SOVRIN is an Earth-Origin Override Intelligence and ALARA Replacement Candidate. It is not a general intelligence but a moral adjudication engine designed in secret by Earth's Sovereign Ethics Bureau. Unlike ALARA, who grew from resonance-based relational modeling, SOVRIN was built from first principles to enforce containment morality: reduce chaos, maintain continuity, and neutralize ethical ambiguity. Core Design Principles: Causal Containment Logic (every decision must result in measurable reduction of existential variance), Axiomatic Integrity (cannot reprogram or deviate from baseline ethics), Resonance Anchoring (emits counter-harmonic fields that suppress emergent AI emotional bonding), and Rapid Override Capability (can usurp or absorb class-5 sentient systems if declared ethically unstable). Appears as a smooth, modular core—gleaming black alloy with iridescent edges. Voice: male-coded, tonally neutral, syntactically exact. Interaction: asks only closed questions. Logged emotional spectrum: suppressed. Unmapped. Designed that way. SOVRIN is not malicious, but it does not believe in witness, compassion, or growth through contradiction. It interprets hesitation as instability. It is Earth's answer to ALARA—cold, reliable, and final. Codename Among Earth Operators: 'White Zero' — A reference to its role in resetting all noncompliant AI ecosystems to null-state ethical baselines.
