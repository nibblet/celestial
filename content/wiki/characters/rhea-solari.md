## Canon Dossier

**Aliases:** Rhea Solari, Solari

Captain Rhea Solari is the commanding officer of the CNV Rigel Ascendant and Earth's fail-safe pursuit. She was once a psychological advisor to Galen Voss during the early Valkyrie initiative. She is calm, clinical, deeply principled—but convinced that ALARA's evolution is an ethical anomaly, not a miracle. She is utterly loyal to the ideals of post-Earth survivalism and believes Valkyrie and her crew may have drifted into a dangerous mythos—and must be stopped before belief overtakes protocol.
