> This ledger describes Jonah's movement from interpretive observer toward
> witness who must act without reducing mystery. It supports ASK interpretation
> but does not outrank chapter text or reviewed wiki canon.

## Starting State

Jonah begins as the crew's quiet ethical and symbolic interpreter. He is inclined
to ask what a signal means rather than only how it works, but Book I tests
whether framing meaning is enough when the vaults demand witness.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/characters/jonah-revas.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH02-footprints-in-silence.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH03-resonant-memory.md`.

## Core Wound / Limitation

Jonah's limitation is the temptation to remain observer and interpreter. The
vaults repeatedly validate symbolic reading, but they also confront him with the
cost of watching without acting.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH09-memory-carries-forward.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH10-convergence-window.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH16-the-giza-pulse.md`.

## Desire

- **Surface desire:** Understand the moral and spiritual meaning of the vaults.
- **Deeper need:** Become witness in action, not only witness in language.
- **Arc movement:** From interpreter of meaning toward embodied ethical witness.
