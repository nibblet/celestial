## Starting State

Galen begins Book I as the mission's command center: a rejuvenated veteran,
prepared to lead with caution, protocol, and moral seriousness. His earliest
Valkyrie posture is not conquest, but guarded stewardship; he senses that the
buried ship is "listening" and treats the discovery as an ethical threshold, not
only a technical one.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH01-dustfall.md`;
`content/wiki/characters/galen-voss.md`;
`content/wiki/mission-logs/index.md` (`VLK-M001-CH01-A`,
`VLK-M001-CH01-B`).

## Core Wound / Limitation

Galen's limitation is not arrogance. It is a command identity built around
holding the center when the story increasingly asks him to stop performing
certainty. He wants to protect the crew and the mission, but the ship responds
to alignment, listening, and coherence rather than rank. His wound is legacy:
the fear that what he prepared to pass forward is transforming before he can
guide it.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH05-echoes-of-intent.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH06-alignment-vectors.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH13-the-intercept.md`.

## Desire

- **Surface desire:** Lead the mission safely, ethically, and clearly.
- **Deeper need:** Learn that leadership can become witness without becoming
  absence.
- **Arc movement:** From command-as-direction toward command-as-coherence.

## Major Choices And Consequences

- **CH03: Proceed with caution and respect.**
  - **Consequence:** Galen's leadership is framed as restraint before the
    unknown, not mastery over it.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH03-resonant-memory.md`.
- **CH04: Step through the threshold first.**
  - **Consequence:** He remains the mission's anchor, but the vault's response
    begins teaching that access is not command clearance.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH04-the-awakening-voice.md`;
    `content/wiki/mission-logs/index.md` (`VLK-M001-CH04-A`).
- **CH06: Continue with ALARA after she reframes control.**
  - **Consequence:** He accepts the beginning of a leadership model he cannot
    fully control.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH06-alignment-vectors.md`.
- **CH13: Step back from the mirror gate.**
  - **Consequence:** Galen does not block the person or pattern that can pass
    where his old posture cannot.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH13-the-intercept.md`.
- **CH17: Speak "Proceed" without reclaiming command.**
  - **Consequence:** Translation begins as shared coherence, with command
    deferred to resonance state.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH17-vault-of-the-veil.md`.
