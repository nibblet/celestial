> This ledger describes Lena's movement from hard classification toward unstable
> participation without abandoning rigor. It supports ASK interpretation but does
> not outrank chapter text or reviewed wiki canon.

## Starting State

Lena begins as the mission's insistence on material discipline: excavation
protocols, stratigraphy, and skepticism toward premature meaning. She wants
evidence to stay evidence, not become myth before it has earned the name.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH01-dustfall.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH02-footprints-in-silence.md`;
`content/wiki/characters/lena-osei.md`.

## Core Wound / Limitation

Lena's limitation is control through classification. Her rigor protects the crew
from fantasy, but the vaults test whether her need to classify can coexist with
participation in a truth that responds to moral and emotional posture.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH02-footprints-in-silence.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH07-harmonic-breach.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH09-memory-carries-forward.md`.

## Desire

- **Surface desire:** Decode the artifact responsibly and keep interpretation
  accountable to evidence.
- **Deeper need:** Accept that instability can be part of honest contact.
- **Arc movement:** From skeptic outside the field toward participant who still
  asks hard questions.
