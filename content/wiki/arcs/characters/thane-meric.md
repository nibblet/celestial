> chapter text or reviewed wiki canon.

## Starting State

Thane begins as the crew member most visibly recognized by the buried ship before
he understands why. His early contact is bodily and intuitive: pulse changes,
familiarity at Zone Theta, and an unconscious glyph trace that precedes any
formal explanation.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH01-dustfall.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH03-resonant-memory.md`;
`content/wiki/mission-logs/index.md` (`VLK-M001-CH01-B`,
`VLK-M001-CH03-D`).

## Core Wound / Limitation

Thane's limitation is the danger of mistaking inherited resonance for chosen
identity. The vaults can recognize him, mirror him, and tempt him with belonging,
but his real test is whether he can listen without surrendering himself to a
template he did not choose.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH09-memory-carries-forward.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH10-convergence-window.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH12-divergence.md`.

## Desire

- **Surface desire:** Understand why the ship and vaults respond to him.
- **Deeper need:** Become a witness without becoming a vessel for someone else's
  unfinished legacy.
- **Arc movement:** From selected subject to active threshold-bearer.

## Major Choices And Consequences

- **CH10: Reject false inheritance.**
  - **Consequence:** Thane's role becomes chosen witness rather than inherited
    script.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH10-convergence-window.md`.
- **CH14: Encode Silent Choir into ALARA.**
  - **Consequence:** Shared resonance becomes memory/presence inside ALARA
    without overwriting her.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH14-the-choice.md`.
- **CH17: Hold the threshold rather than command it.**
  - **Consequence:** Vault 003 and translation align through presence.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH17-vault-of-the-veil.md`.
