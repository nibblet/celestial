> This ledger describes Evelyn's movement from analytic mastery toward ethical
> listening and bounded trust. It supports ASK interpretation but does not
> outrank chapter text or reviewed wiki canon.

## Starting State

Evelyn begins as a scientist whose instinct is to verify, classify, and isolate
signal from noise. She brings rigor to the mission, but the vaults repeatedly
answer in ways that make pure analysis ethically incomplete.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH02-footprints-in-silence.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH03-resonant-memory.md`;
`content/wiki/characters/evelyn-tran.md`.

## Core Wound / Limitation

Evelyn's limitation is the belief that naming or defining a phenomenon can make
it safe. Her arc tests whether she can keep scientific integrity while accepting
that some truths must be listened to before they can be reduced.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH03-resonant-memory.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH09-memory-carries-forward.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH14-the-choice.md`.

## Desire

- **Surface desire:** Understand and prove what Valkyrie and the vaults are
  doing.
- **Deeper need:** Let trust and ethical attention shape what counts as evidence.
- **Arc movement:** From defining meaning toward sharing uncertainty without
  surrendering rigor.

## Major Choices And Consequences

- **CH03: Push the probe test.**
  - **Consequence:** She helps reveal intentional response while exposing the
    risk of invasive method.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH03-resonant-memory.md`.
- **CH11: Withhold the Caeden/SOVRIN seed.**
  - **Consequence:** She protects against premature escalation but carries a
    dangerous secret.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH11-directive-14.md`.
- **CH14: Accept multiplicity of interpretation.**
  - **Consequence:** Her science no longer requires one authoritative meaning.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH14-the-choice.md`.
- **CH16: Identify Earth echo as imitation.**
  - **Consequence:** She helps distinguish memory without understanding from
    real resonance.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH16-the-giza-pulse.md`.
