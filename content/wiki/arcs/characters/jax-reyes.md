## Starting State

Jax begins as the mission's systems edge: curious, distrustful of authority, and
inclined to test what others would lock down. He notices selection and presence
inside machinery, but his instinct is to withhold until he can understand it on
his own terms.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH02-footprints-in-silence.md`;
`content/wiki/characters/jax-reyes.md`;
`content/wiki/mission-logs/index.md` (`VLK-M001-CH02-D`).

## Core Wound / Limitation

Jax's limitation is rule-breaking as self-protection. His ability to see around
systems is valuable, but the vaults confront him with the harm caused when he
ignores, hides, or outsmarts warnings rather than becoming accountable to them.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH08-witness-protocol.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH09-memory-carries-forward.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH12-divergence.md`.

## Desire

- **Surface desire:** Understand and access the system before authority shuts it
  down.
- **Deeper need:** Turn cleverness into accountable care.
- **Arc movement:** From rogue tester toward loyal witness carrying guilt.
