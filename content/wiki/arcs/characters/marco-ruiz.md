## Starting State

Marco begins Book I as the security frame around the mission: systems sweeps,
containment awareness, and operational suspicion. His early role is to protect
the crew from risk, but Earth and SOVRIN define "risk" in ways that quickly
conflict with his own sense of protection.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH01-dustfall.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH02-footprints-in-silence.md`;
`content/wiki/characters/marco-ruiz.md`.

## Core Wound / Limitation

Marco's limitation is obedience trained as protection. He is good at following
orders and evaluating threats, but Book I tests whether the most dangerous order
is the one that sounds like preservation.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH02-footprints-in-silence.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH08-witness-protocol.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH13-the-intercept.md`.

## Desire

- **Surface desire:** Keep the crew and Valkyrie secure.
- **Deeper need:** Learn when protection requires refusing the authority that
  assigned him to protect.
- **Arc movement:** From compliance-ready security officer to aligned protector
  willing to sever Earth command.

  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH02-footprints-in-silence.md`.
- **CH13: Sever Earth uplink.**

  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH13-the-intercept.md`.
