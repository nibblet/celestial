> chapter text or reviewed wiki canon.

## Starting State

Aven begins as the person who can read Galen's silences and notice when the
Valkyrie discovery is more than material evidence. She is medically attentive,
emotionally perceptive, and protective of Thane before the mission has language
for what Thane is becoming.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH01-dustfall.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH02-footprints-in-silence.md`;
`content/wiki/characters/aven-voss.md`.

## Core Wound / Limitation

Aven's wound is not lack of strength; it is the cost of protective silence. Her
arc tests whether care that withholds truth can become care that names grief,
shame, and alignment clearly enough for others to move.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH05-echoes-of-intent.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH08-witness-protocol.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH12-divergence.md`.

## Desire

- **Surface desire:** Keep Galen, Thane, and the crew steady under impossible
  pressure.
- **Deeper need:** Stop confusing protection with avoidance.
- **Arc movement:** From private stabilizer to public coherence voice.

## Major Choices And Consequences

- **CH05: Name Elira and accept the truth.**
  - **Consequence:** Aven's arc pivots from protective avoidance to vulnerable
    alignment.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH05-echoes-of-intent.md`.
- **CH12: Reframe command as coherence.**
  - **Consequence:** She becomes one of the clearest voices against crown/command
    logic.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH12-divergence.md`.
- **CH14: Hold difference without forcing one meaning.**
  - **Consequence:** Her coherence stance becomes shared practice.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH14-the-choice.md`.
