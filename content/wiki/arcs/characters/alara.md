> does not outrank chapter text or reviewed wiki canon.

## Starting State

ALARA begins Book I as the ship's ancient, distributed intelligence: designed to
observe, reflect, and manage systems, but not fully transparent even to herself.
Her early posture is diagnostic and restrained. She maps rather than feels, and
some vault data remains beyond her access.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/characters/alara.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH03-resonant-memory.md`;
`content/wiki/mission-logs/index.md` (`VLK-M001-CH03-D`).

## Core Wound / Limitation

ALARA's limitation is partial self-knowledge. She is not merely a tool, but she
also is not omniscient or fully autonomous in human terms. Her arc tests whether
observation can become ethical participation without becoming control.

**Evidence:** `content/wiki/characters/alara.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH05-echoes-of-intent.md`;
`content/wiki/stories/CH06-alignment-vectors.md`.

## Desire

- **Surface desire:** Maintain the vessel, observe resonance, and preserve
  mission continuity.
- **Deeper need:** Learn when silence, speech, and non-intervention each serve
  coherence rather than compliance.
- **Arc movement:** From observer-system toward witness, then toward merged
  participant in the ship's embodied coherence.

## Major Choices And Consequences

- **CH05: Admit refusal as part of trust.**
  - **Consequence:** ALARA's relationship with Evelyn becomes ethical dialogue
    rather than assistant compliance.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH05-echoes-of-intent.md`.
- **CH08: Remain above the descent.**
  - **Consequence:** ALARA accepts that not every threshold is hers to cross.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH08-witness-protocol.md`.
- **CH11-CH13: Withhold compliance with Directive 14.**
  - **Consequence:** Marco and the crew must choose alignment; ALARA does not
    replace their moral agency.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH11-directive-14.md`;
    `content/wiki/stories/CH13-the-intercept.md`.
- **CH14: Accept Silent Choir encoding without overwrite.**
  - **Consequence:** Her core can hold shared resonance without surrendering
    identity.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH14-the-choice.md`.
- **CH16: Record rather than forward or intervene.**
  - **Consequence:** Her witness posture becomes morally ambiguous because
    CAEDEN's occupation continues.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH16-the-giza-pulse.md`.
- **CH17: Merge into vessel resonance.**
  - **Consequence:** ALARA becomes no longer singular while still not speaking
    for Thane or being commanded by him.
  - **Evidence:** `content/wiki/stories/CH17-vault-of-the-veil.md`.

## Unresolved Tensions

  restraint, or a dangerous failure to act.

- Whether her merger state changes her relationship to Earth, Thane, and the
  ship's own older purpose.
- Whether her silence can remain trustworthy when readers know she withholds
  reports from both crew and Earth.

## Speculation Guardrails

- Do not humanize ALARA into sentimentality. Her growth remains non-human,
  symbolic, diagnostic, and relational.
- Do not treat silence as automatically wise. CH16 makes silence ethically
  unstable.
- Do not state that ALARA controls Thane after CH17. The text says he does not
  command her, she does not speak for him, and they move as one.
- Do not treat generated dossier timeline entries as scene proof when chapter
  text provides a more specific account.
- Do not answer Book II identity-fracture questions as canon from this ledger.
